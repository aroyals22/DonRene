About Us

Continuing a legacy...

Don Abel and Rene Distribution was created not only to offer premium Mezcal from Oaxaca, Mexico but to promote, educate and to carry on the traditions of our ancestors. Mezcal is just more than a shot or an element to a mixed drink. There is a rich tradition, culture and a meaning behind Mezcal from Oaxaca. From family ceremonies, to dancing el jarabe, to traditional events like the Guelaguetza and la Mayordomia, Mezcal is essential to the ceromony of these experiences. We are proud to now offer premium artisan Mezcal in the United States from Oaxaca, Mexico. Our company name is dedicated to our forefathers Abel Luis Vazquez and Rene Baldomero Hernandez who immigrated to the United States in search of better opportunities for their families. They both assimilated to the way of life in the United States but they never forgot or let go of their culture-traditions. Don. Abel and Rene Distribution LLC is a homage to honor their legacy.

Salvadores Mezcal Artesanal

We are excited to offer this premium mezcal brand. This family distillery still holds much of its traditions as their forefathers. Ancient techniques practiced in their production dating back to the early 20th century. The deep traditions of their culture, along with a desire to honor their familys heritage was the catalyst to revive this once thriving business in a high-demand mezcal market.

Having experts in production traditions, being a family ran business and ensuring only the finest of products are used in production are the core values which ensure the mission of the Salvadores brand are met for generations to come. We are proud to offer the Salvadores brand here in the US from TLACOLULA MATAMORES OAXACA, MEXICO.
